ColorMatch

Instructions to play

1.Use your mouse or your trackpad on the area to move your player.

2.You have to pass the obstacles .

3.If there are no obstacles just go.

4.If the obstacles are of the same color as the player they will let you go.

5.If the obstacles are of the different color they will block and you will get out.

6.The color of the tiles and the player will change on hitting the wall.

7.Pass more and more number of obstacles to increase your score.

Enjoy the game !!
